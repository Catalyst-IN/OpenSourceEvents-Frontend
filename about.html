ABOUT US

Most of the times students want to participate in the events like Open-Source Events & Hackathons but they don't know the dates or the event details. This is a huge problem faced by majority .So we have come up with an idea why not to enlist all the details of such events in a single website ?

Thus we had created a website that contains a list of Open-Source Events and Hackathons. They are in proper month wise timeline so that the user does not face difficulty while searching through the events. This site contains links and details of the events so that no Student will have difficulty in finding them.

You can reach the maintainers and our community on Catalyst-Discord. If you are interested in contributing to the OpenSourceEvents-Frontend, We have a dedicated stream for this Project #OpenSourceEvents-Frontend, where you can ask questions and interact with the community, join with us!
